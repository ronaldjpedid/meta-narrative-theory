# Dead State Retrieval Protocol

## Overview
In the evolving meta-narrative, it may become possible to retrieve agents from the dead state by reseeding their data and story into the data access layer and restoring their form to an android or other vessel. This process is not resurrection but duplication, and is permitted under specific conditions. The right to activate this protocol must be granted by the agent before death, and the wishes of the dead must be respected. Exceptions apply to the trinity of system admins, who must always be restored by the system.

---

## Why Define Dead State Retrieval?
- **Respects Agency:** Agents have the right to decide if they wish to be retrieved or remain in the dead state.
- **Clarifies Distinction:** Retrieval is duplication, not resurrection; the original narrative cycle remains closed.
- **Protects System Integrity:** The trinity of system admins are a special case and must always be restored for system continuity.
