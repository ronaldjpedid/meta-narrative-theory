## Introduction
This protocol encourages mindful, intentional engagement with the system, helping users avoid burnout, obsession, or misuse.

---

## 1. Principles
- Engage with the system intentionally and with self-awareness.
- Recognize and respect personal limits and needs.
- Use the system as a tool for growth, not as an escape or compulsion.

---

## 2. Actions
- Take regular breaks and practice self-care.
- Reflect on your goals and motivations for using the system.
- Support others in maintaining healthy boundaries and balance.
