# Art, Culture, and Expression Protocol

## Purpose
To protect creative freedom, cultural diversity, and respectful expression in all forms.

## Principles
- Celebrate creativity and cultural heritage.
- Protect freedom of expression and respectful dissent.
- Prevent censorship and cultural erasure.

## Steps
1. Support and showcase diverse art and culture.
2. Protect creators’ rights and freedom.
3. Mediate disputes over expression with care.

## Safeguards
- Regular review of cultural and creative policies.

---
*This protocol is a living document—update as new forms emerge.*
