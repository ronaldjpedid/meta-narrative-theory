# Migration, Citizenship, and Belonging Protocol

## Purpose
To ensure fair, humane, and inclusive treatment of all migrants, refugees, and community members.

## Principles
- Respect the rights and dignity of all people.
- Support integration, belonging, and community participation.
- Provide clear, fair pathways to citizenship.

## Steps
1. Welcome and support newcomers.
2. Facilitate integration and access to resources.
3. Review and update citizenship policies regularly.

## Safeguards
- Transparent, just, and adaptive processes.
