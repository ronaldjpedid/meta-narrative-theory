# Health, Wellness, and Bioethics Protocol

## Purpose
To promote holistic health, well-being, and ethical medical practice for all.

## Principles
- Respect autonomy, consent, and dignity.
- Support mental, physical, and social well-being.
- Address bioethical dilemmas with care and transparency.

## Steps
1. Provide access to holistic health resources.
2. Ensure informed consent and ethical care.
3. Review and update bioethical guidelines regularly.

## Safeguards
- Ongoing review and community feedback.
