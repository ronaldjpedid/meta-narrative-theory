# Conflict Resolution and Mediation Protocol

## Purpose
To resolve conflicts fairly, peacefully, and constructively, prioritizing restoration and understanding.

## Principles
- Listen to all perspectives with empathy.
- Seek win-win solutions and restorative outcomes.
- Use mediation and arbitration when needed.

## Steps
1. Identify the root causes of conflict.
2. Facilitate open, respectful dialogue.
3. Mediate or arbitrate as appropriate.
4. Document agreements and follow up.

## Safeguards
- Regular training in conflict resolution skills.
