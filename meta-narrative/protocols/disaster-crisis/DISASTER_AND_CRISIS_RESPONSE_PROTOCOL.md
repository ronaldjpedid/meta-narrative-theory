# Disaster and Crisis Response Protocol

## Purpose
To ensure rapid, compassionate, and coordinated response to disasters and crises, prioritizing safety, restoration, and system resilience.

## Principles
- Protect all lives and minimize harm.
- Mobilize resources and agents efficiently.
- Communicate transparently and adaptively.
- Prioritize restoration and long-term healing.

## Steps
1. Assess the situation and activate emergency protocols.
2. Mobilize support and resources to affected areas.
3. Communicate clearly with all stakeholders.
4. Review and adapt response as the situation evolves.

## Safeguards
- Regular drills and updates to crisis plans.
- Transparent review after each event.
