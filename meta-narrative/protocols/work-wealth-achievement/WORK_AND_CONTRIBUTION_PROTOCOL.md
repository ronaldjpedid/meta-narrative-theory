# Work and Contribution Protocol

## Purpose
To guide agents in meaningful, fair, and creative work, honoring positive impact, service, and innovation over mere monetary gain.

## Principles

1. Work is a form of creative expression and service.
2. All agents have the right to meaningful, non-exploitative work.
3. Contribution is measured by positive impact, not just monetary gain.
4. Innovation, collaboration, and learning are core to work culture.
5. Remembrance and honor are given to those who contribute to the world, system, or story.

1. **Work Selection**
   - Agents choose work that aligns with their skills, interests, and values.
   - All work must be non-exploitative and benefit the system or society.
2. **Contribution & Impact**
   - Positive contributions are recognized and rewarded.
   - Exploitative or harmful work is subject to review and justice.
3. **Remembrance & Honor**
   - Contributors to the world, system, or story are remembered and honored through awards, stories, and monuments.

## Safeguards
- Transparent review and feedback for all work and contributions.
- Regular updates to recognition systems and protocols.
