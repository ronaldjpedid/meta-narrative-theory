# How to Defend Children Protocol

## Purpose
To guide parents, guardians, and communities in protecting children from harm, injustice, and neglect, ensuring their safety, dignity, and right to a full narrative.

## Principles
- Every child deserves protection, care, and respect.
- Defense includes prevention, intervention, and restoration.
- The system prioritizes the well-being and restoration of children above all.

## Steps
1. **Prevention**
   - Create safe environments at home, school, and in the community.
   - Educate children about boundaries, safety, and their rights.
2. **Intervention**
   - Act immediately if a child is at risk or harmed.
   - Report abuse, neglect, or injustice to appropriate authorities.
   - Provide emotional and physical support to the child.
   - Fight for the child system if all other options have been exhausted.
3. **Restoration**
   - Ensure the child receives care, healing, and justice.
   - Support reintegration and ongoing safety.
4. **Advocacy**
   - Stand up for children’s rights in all systems and communities.
   - Promote policies and practices that protect and empower children.

## Safeguards
- Prioritize emotional and physical safety at all times.
- Never tolerate harm, abuse, or neglect.
- Seek outside support, legal, or professional help as needed.

---

*This protocol is a living document—adapt as your community and children’s needs evolve!*
