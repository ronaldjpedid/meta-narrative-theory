# Technology and AI Ethics Protocol

## Purpose
To ensure all technology and AI systems align with ethical ideals, transparency, and human flourishing.

## Principles
- Prioritize safety, transparency, and alignment.
- Respect autonomy and prevent harm.
- Enable oversight and fail-safes.

## Steps
1. Audit all AI and tech for alignment and safety.
2. Provide transparency and explainability.
3. Enable user control and recourse.

## Safeguards
- Regular ethics reviews and updates.
