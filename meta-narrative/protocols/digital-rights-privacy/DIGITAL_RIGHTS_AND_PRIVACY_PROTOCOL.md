# Digital Rights and Privacy Protocol

## Purpose
To protect digital rights, privacy, and autonomy for all agents and users in the system.

## Principles
- Respect consent and data ownership.
- Ensure transparency in data use and AI actions.
- Provide the right to erasure and digital identity control.

## Steps
1. Obtain informed consent for all data collection and use.
2. Allow users to access, modify, or erase their data.
3. Regularly audit and update privacy safeguards.

## Safeguards
- Transparent privacy policies and regular audits.
