## Introduction
This protocol encourages the regular practice and expression of gratitude within the system, fostering a culture of appreciation, support, and positive reinforcement.

---

## 1. Principles
- Acknowledge and thank others for their contributions, growth, and support.
- Celebrate milestones, achievements, and acts of kindness.
- Use gratitude to build trust, resilience, and community.

---

## 2. Actions
- Express thanks in words, writing, or actions.
- Recognize both big and small contributions.
- Reflect on what you are grateful for regularly.
