## Introduction
This protocol guides the process of forgiveness, repair, and restoration after mistakes or harm, focusing on learning, healing, and second chances.

---

## 1. Principles
- Encourage forgiveness for oneself and others.
- Prioritize restoration and learning over punishment or blame.
- Support second chances and ongoing growth.

---

## 2. Actions
- Acknowledge harm and take responsibility.
- Seek and offer forgiveness genuinely.
- Work together to repair and restore relationships.
