## Introduction
This protocol ensures the proactive inclusion of diverse voices, perspectives, and experiences, making everyone feel seen, valued, and empowered.

## 1. Principles
- Welcome and celebrate diversity in all forms.
- Proactively include marginalized and underrepresented voices.
- Foster an environment where everyone can contribute and thrive.

---

## 2. Actions
- Seek out and amplify diverse perspectives.
- Address and remove barriers to participation.
- Regularly review and update practices for greater inclusion.
