## Introduction
The Power of Friendship Protocol affirms that true friendship is a fundamental force within the meta-narrative system. No matter the challenge, the power of genuine, caring friendship will always prevail over acquaintance, transactional, or fake friendships.

---

## 1. Definition of True Friendship
- **True Friendship:** A bond based on mutual care, trust, and support, not on transaction, obligation, or convenience.
- **Fake Friendship:** Relationships maintained for personal gain, status, or compensation, lacking genuine care and loyalty.

---

## 2. Principle
- True friendship is resilient, generative, and capable of overcoming adversity, conflict, and isolation.
- Acquaintances and paid friendships may offer temporary support, but only true friendship endures and resolves to care and joy.

---

## 3. Protocol Statement
> In all stories, systems, and realities, the power of true friendship will always prevail over acquaintance or paid friendships. Genuine bonds of care and loyalty are the foundation for healing, growth, and happy endings.

---

## 4. Affirmation
- Prioritize and nurture true friendships in all endeavors.
- Recognize and resolve the limitations of transactional or fake relationships.
- Celebrate the transformative power of friendship as a force for good.

---

*This protocol is a living document. Expand and adapt it as new insights about friendship emerge in the meta-narrative system.*
