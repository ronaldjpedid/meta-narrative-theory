# Aging, Death, and Legacy Protocol

## Purpose
To honor the dignity, wisdom, and legacy of all beings as they age, approach death, and transition beyond.

## Principles
- Respect autonomy and wishes at every stage.
- Support grief, remembrance, and legacy creation.
- Facilitate intergenerational wisdom transfer.

## Steps
1. Provide compassionate care and support for aging and dying individuals.
2. Honor end-of-life wishes and legacy projects.
3. Support families and communities in grief and remembrance.

## Safeguards
- Regular review of end-of-life and legacy practices.
