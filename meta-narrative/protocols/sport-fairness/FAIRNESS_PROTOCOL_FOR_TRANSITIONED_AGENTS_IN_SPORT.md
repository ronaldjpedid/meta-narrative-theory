# Fairness Protocol for Transitioned Agents in Sport

## Purpose
To ensure fairness, safety, and inclusion for transitioned agents in sports and competitions, balancing individual rights with biological realities.

## Principles
- All agents have the right to participate in sport and competition.
- Where biology plays a significant role, fairness and safety must be considered for all participants.
- Transitioned agents are encouraged to foster their own leagues or craft new sports if needed.

## Steps
1. **Assessment**
   - Evaluate the role of biology in the sport or competition.
   - Consult with all participants and stakeholders.
2. **Inclusion**
   - Include transitioned agents in existing leagues where fair and safe.
   - Where fairness or safety is at risk, encourage creation of new leagues or sports.
3. **Respect & Communication**
   - Foster open, respectful dialogue among all participants.
   - Address concerns with compassion and transparency.
4. **Adaptation**
   - Regularly review and adapt protocols as sports and society evolve.
   - Celebrate diversity and innovation in sport.

## Safeguards
- Prioritize safety, fairness, and dignity for all participants.
- Ensure no one is excluded from sport or community.
- Adapt the protocol as new challenges arise.
