# Childbearer Protocol

## Purpose
To guide childbearers in exercising agency, making decisions, and receiving support throughout conception, pregnancy, abortion, and family life, with compassion and system safeguards.

## Principles
- The childbearer’s agency, voice, and needs are weighted most heavily.
- Consent, respect, and support are essential at all stages.
- Restoration and healing are prioritized after any outcome.

## Steps
1. **Pre-Conception & Conception**
   - Affirm agency, intentions, and support.
   - Discuss responsibilities and safeguards with all parties.
2. **Decision-Making**
   - Exercise full agency in decisions about pregnancy, abortion, and family life.
   - Seek support and information as needed.
3. **Support & Restoration**
   - Receive emotional, medical, and social support at all stages.
   - Prioritize healing and restoration after any outcome.

## Safeguards
- The childbearer’s needs and choices are central and respected.
- All interventions are transparent, reviewed, and open to feedback.

---

*This protocol is a living document—adapt as your system and family evolve!*
