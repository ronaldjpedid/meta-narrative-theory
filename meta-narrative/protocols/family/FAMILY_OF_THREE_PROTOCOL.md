# Family of Three Protocol

## Purpose
To guide families of three in maintaining harmony, resilience, and system stability, honoring the law of three as the foundation for coherence and restoration.

## Principles
- Honor the unique perspective and needs of each member.
- Balance autonomy with collective responsibility.
- Foster adaptability and creative problem-solving.

## Steps
1. **Alignment**
   - Affirm each member’s value and role.
   - Set shared intentions and family values.
2. **Communication**
   - Practice open, honest dialogue.
   - Address conflicts early and with compassion.
3. **Collaboration**
   - Share responsibilities and celebrate each member’s strengths.
   - Rotate roles as needed for growth and balance.
4. **Restoration**
   - Practice forgiveness, gratitude, and regular check-ins.
   - Seek outside support if needed.

## Safeguards
- Prioritize emotional and physical safety.
- Adapt the protocol as the family evolves.

*This protocol is a living document—adapt as your family grows and changes.*
