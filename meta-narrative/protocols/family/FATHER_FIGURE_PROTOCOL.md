# Father-Figure Protocol

## Purpose
To guide father-figures in supporting, protecting, and sharing responsibility in conception, creation, and family life, with compassion, agency, and system safeguards.

## Principles
- Father-figures have agency, responsibility, and a duty to support the childbearer and child.
- Consent, respect, and open communication are essential.
- Restoration and healing are prioritized after any loss or conflict.

## Steps
1. **Pre-Conception & Conception**
   - Affirm intentions, responsibilities, and support for the childbearer.
   - Ensure ongoing consent and respect.
2. **Support & Protection**
   - Provide emotional, financial, and physical support as needed.
   - Share responsibility for the child’s well-being and family stability.
3. **Restoration & Healing**
   - Offer compassion and support after any loss, conflict, or change.
   - Seek restoration and growth for all parties.

## Safeguards
- Agency and consent are paramount.
- All interventions are transparent, reviewed, and open to feedback.

---

*This protocol is a living document—adapt as your system and family evolve!*
