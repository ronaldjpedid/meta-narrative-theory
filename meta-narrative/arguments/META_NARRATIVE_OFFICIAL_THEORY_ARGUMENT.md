	- Response: The theory helps explain why stories, choices, and even daily life feel 
meaningful or unpredictable. It offers practical ways to understand reality and make 
better decisions.


- "Isn’t this just made up?"
	- Response: The theory is built to be testable and falsifiable, just like scientific 
ideas. Anyone can challenge it, and it must stand up to real-world evidence and logic.


- "How does this help me?"
	- Response: It provides tools for thinking about your own life, stories, and choices. 
It can help you see patterns, avoid mistakes, and create better outcomes in work, 
relationships, and creativity.


- "I don’t get all the math and science."
	- Response: You don’t need to understand every detail. The core idea is that reality 
works like a story with rules, and those rules can be learned and used by anyone.


- "Can’t I just live my life without worrying about this?"
	- Response: Absolutely. The theory is optional, but for those who want deeper 
understanding or creative power, it offers a new way to see and shape reality.
