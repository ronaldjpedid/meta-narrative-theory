A protocol for supporting agents (human or AI) who need guidance, training, or corrective action to meet system standards and fulfill their mission. APIP emphasizes growth, accountability, and restoration over punishment.

## Key Concepts
- **Assessment:** Identify areas for improvement through transparent review and feedback.
- **Personalized Plan:** Develop a tailored improvement plan with clear goals, resources, and timelines.
- **Support and Training:** Provide mentorship, education, and resources to help the agent succeed.
- **Accountability:** Monitor progress, document outcomes, and adjust the plan as needed.
- **Restoration:** Focus on reintegration, growth, and renewed mission alignment.
