# TECH_BRO — Technology-Enabled Control, Harm, & Behavioral Regulation Operator

**TECH_BRO** stands for **Technology-Enabled Control, Harm, & Behavioral Regulation Operator**. This acronym describes individuals or groups who use technology to instill fear, manipulate behavior, and maintain control—often prioritizing profit, surveillance, and power over love, agency, and restoration.

## Characteristics
- Promotes fear-based solutions and narratives
- Uses technology for surveillance, manipulation, and exploitation
- Prioritizes control and profit over community and care
- Undermines agency, privacy, and trust

## Contrast with Meta-Narrative Ideals
- Meta-narrative theory advocates for technology as a tool for love, agency, restoration, and universal benefit—not fear or control.
