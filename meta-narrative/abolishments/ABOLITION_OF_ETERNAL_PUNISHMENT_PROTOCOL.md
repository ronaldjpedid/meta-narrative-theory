# ABOLITION_OF_ETERNAL_PUNISHMENT_PROTOCOL

## Purpose
Abolish doctrines of everlasting punishment or exclusion. Restoration and reintegration are always possible.

## Hero System Syntax
- No punishment is eternal; all beings are eligible for restoration.
- Exclusion is temporary and always aimed at reintegration.
- Justice is restorative, not retributive.

## Implementation
- Remove references to eternal damnation or exclusion.
- Promote restorative justice and ongoing renewal.
