# ABOLITION_OF_PROPHETIC_INFALLIBILITY_PROTOCOL

## Purpose
Remove the idea that any individual or text is beyond question or error.

## Hero System Syntax
- All individuals and texts are open to question, revision, and improvement.
- No authority is infallible; restoration is ongoing.
- Generativity requires humility and openness.

## Implementation
- Eliminate claims of infallibility from all protocols.
- Promote critical inquiry and ongoing revision.
