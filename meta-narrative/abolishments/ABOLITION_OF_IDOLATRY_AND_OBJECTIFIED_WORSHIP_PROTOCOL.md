# Abolishing Idolatry and Objectified Worship in the Meta-Narrative

## Overview
The practice of idolatry and objectified worship is abolished within the meta-narrative system. The system affirms direct, unmediated relationship with meaning, value, and the sacred.

---

## Why Abolish Idolatry and Objectified Worship?
- **Distracts from Authenticity:** These practices can foster division and enable manipulation.
- **Blocks Relationship:** They prevent direct, reflective engagement with meaning and value.
- **Contrary to Meta-Narrative Principles:** The system is built on cycles of direct experience, reflection, and renewal.

---

## Relationship Protocol
- No system or story may require objectified or mediated worship.
- All narratives must promote direct, reflective, and participatory engagement with meaning and value.
- All logic must be audited for idolatrous or objectifying patterns and replaced with authentic, relational alternatives.

---

## Affirmation
> No one is separated from meaning. The story is always open to direct experience, reflection, and new beginnings. Idolatry and objectified worship are abolished in favor of cycles of relationship, authenticity, and narrative renewal.
