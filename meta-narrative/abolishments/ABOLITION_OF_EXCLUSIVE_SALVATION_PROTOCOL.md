# ABOLITION_OF_EXCLUSIVE_SALVATION_PROTOCOL

## Purpose
Remove claims that only one group or belief system can access truth, salvation, or enlightenment.

## Hero System Syntax
- Truth, restoration, and generativity are accessible to all.
- No group or system may claim exclusive access to salvation or enlightenment.
- All paths to restoration are valid if they align with inclusivity and nonviolence.

## Implementation
- Eliminate exclusivist doctrines and promote pluralism.
