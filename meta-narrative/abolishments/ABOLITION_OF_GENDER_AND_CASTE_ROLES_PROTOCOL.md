# ABOLITION_OF_GENDER_AND_CASTE_ROLES_PROTOCOL

## Purpose
Remove fixed roles or hierarchies based on gender, caste, or birth.

## Hero System Syntax
- All roles are open to all individuals, regardless of gender, caste, or birth.
- Hierarchies must be flexible, inclusive, and generative.
- Restoration and participation are universal rights.

## Implementation
- Eliminate gender and caste-based roles from all protocols.
- Promote universal access and flexible participation.
