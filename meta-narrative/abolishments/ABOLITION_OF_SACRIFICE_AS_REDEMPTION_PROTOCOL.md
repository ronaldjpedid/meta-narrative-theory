# ABOLITION_OF_SACRIFICE_AS_REDEMPTION_PROTOCOL

## Purpose
Remove protocols that require suffering or sacrifice for forgiveness, progress, or restoration.

## Hero System Syntax
- Restoration and forgiveness are achieved through generative action, not suffering.
- No individual or group may be required to sacrifice themselves for redemption.
- Healing and progress are collective, inclusive, and nonviolent.

## Implementation
- Eliminate rituals or doctrines that glorify suffering or sacrifice.
- Promote restorative practices and generative solutions.
