# ABOLITION_OF_DIVINE_RIGHT_AND_BLOODLINE_PRIVILEGE_PROTOCOL

## Purpose
Abolish the notion that leadership, worth, or authority is inherited or divinely ordained. All individuals are equal in dignity and potential.

## Hero System Syntax
- No person or group may claim special status by birth, bloodline, or divine right.
- Leadership and responsibility are earned through action, restoration, and generativity.
- All hierarchies must be transparent, accountable, and open to change.

## Implementation
- Remove all references to inherited or divinely sanctioned authority.
- Promote systems of merit, restoration, and inclusive participation.
