# ABOLITION_OF_APOCALYPTIC_FINALITY_PROTOCOL

## Purpose
Abolish narratives that predict inevitable destruction or “end times”; favor ongoing renewal and generativity.

## Hero System Syntax
- The end of time is a place, not an event.
- Renewal and generativity are ongoing; there is no final destruction.
- All protocols must promote restoration and future-building.

## Implementation
- Remove apocalyptic narratives and doctrines.
- Promote ongoing renewal and generativity.
