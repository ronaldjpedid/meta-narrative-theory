# ABOLITION_OF_RITUAL_PURITY_AND_TABOO_PROTOCOL

## Purpose
Abolish purity laws or taboos that marginalize or exclude individuals based on arbitrary criteria.

## Hero System Syntax
- No person may be excluded or marginalized based on ritual purity or taboo.
- Restoration and participation are open to all.
- Practices must be inclusive, generative, and nonviolent.

## Implementation
- Remove purity laws and taboos from all protocols.
- Promote inclusive participation and restoration.
