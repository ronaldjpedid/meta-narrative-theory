# ABOLITION_OF_MARTYRDOM_GLORIFICATION_PROTOCOL

## Purpose
Abolish the glorification of dying for a cause; prioritize living values and restoration.

## Hero System Syntax
- Living generatively is valued above dying for a cause.
- Restoration and progress are achieved through life, not death.
- No protocol may glorify martyrdom or self-sacrifice.

## Implementation
- Remove martyrdom glorification from all guides and protocols.
- Promote living values and generative action.
