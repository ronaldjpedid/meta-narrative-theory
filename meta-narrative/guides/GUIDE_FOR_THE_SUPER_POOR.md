# Guide for the Super Poor

## Meta-Narrative Principles for the Super Poor
- Every day is a story of resilience and hope.
- Embrace duality: struggle and strength, scarcity and resourcefulness.
- Life is recursive—each challenge builds wisdom and courage.

## Practical Insights
- Use feedback: seek support, adapt, and learn from every experience.
- Restoration: setbacks are not the end—every moment is a chance for renewal.
- Agency: your choices, no matter how small, matter in your story.

## Inspiration
- You are a survivor and a teacher. Your journey inspires empathy, change, and new beginnings.
