# Guide for Teachers

## Meta-Narrative Principles for Teachers
- Every lesson is a seed—branching into countless futures.
- Embrace duality: structure and creativity, guidance and autonomy.
- Teaching is a recursive act; each student’s growth echoes through generations.

## Practical Insights
- Foster agency: let students make choices and learn from outcomes.
- Use feedback: adapt your methods to meet diverse needs.
- Restoration: mistakes are learning opportunities for both teacher and student.

## Inspiration
- You shape the future by empowering others. Your story is woven into every life you touch.
