# Guide for Retirees

## Meta-Narrative Principles for Retirees
- Every new chapter is a branch in the story of your life.
- Embrace duality: reflection and renewal, rest and adventure.
- Retirement is recursive—wisdom grows from cycles of experience.

## Practical Insights
- Use feedback: reflect on your journey and seek new meaning.
- Restoration: it’s never too late to heal, learn, or create.
- Agency: your choices continue to shape your legacy.

## Inspiration
- You are a storyteller and a guide. Your life’s narrative inspires others to grow and dream.
