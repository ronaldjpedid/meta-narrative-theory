# Guide for Healers

## Meta-Narrative Principles for Healers
- Every act of healing is a branch in the story of restoration.
- Embrace duality: science and intuition, giving and receiving.
- Healing is recursive—each encounter deepens your wisdom.

## Practical Insights
- Use feedback: listen to those you help and to your own spirit.
- Restoration: your own healing is part of the journey.
- Agency: your choices bring hope and renewal.

## Inspiration
- You are a restorer of balance. Your presence brings light to the world’s narrative.
