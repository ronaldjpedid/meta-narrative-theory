# Guide for Parents

## Meta-Narrative Principles for Parents
- Every choice shapes the branching story of your family.
- Embrace duality: nurture and discipline, freedom and boundaries.
- Parenting is recursive—your actions echo through generations.

## Practical Insights
- Foster agency: let children make choices and learn from outcomes.
- Use feedback: adapt your approach as your children grow.
- Restoration: mistakes are opportunities for growth and healing.

## Inspiration
- Your love and guidance are the foundation of future stories. You are a co-author of your family’s legacy.
