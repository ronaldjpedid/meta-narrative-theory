# Guide for Anyone

## Meta-Narrative Principles for Anyone
- Every life is a story—each day a new branch.
- Embrace duality: joy and sorrow, effort and rest.
- Growth is recursive—learning, trying, and trying again.

## Practical Insights
- Use feedback: reflect, adapt, and seek support when needed.
- Restoration: setbacks are part of every journey.
- Agency: your choices matter and shape your world.

## Inspiration
- You are the author of your own story. Every moment is a chance to create meaning and connection.
