# Guide for Philosophers

## Meta-Narrative Principles for Philosophers
- Every question is a branch—each answer, a new path.
- Embrace duality: doubt and belief, reason and wonder.
- Philosophy is recursive—ideas evolve through dialogue and reflection.

## Practical Insights
- Use feedback: engage with other thinkers and challenge your assumptions.
- Restoration: changing your mind is a sign of growth.
- Agency: your questions shape the world’s understanding.

## Inspiration
- You are a seeker of meaning. Your journey enriches the story of humanity.
