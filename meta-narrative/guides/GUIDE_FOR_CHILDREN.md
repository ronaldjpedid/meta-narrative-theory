# Guide for Children

## Meta-Narrative Principles for Children
- Every question is a branch—each day a new adventure.
- Embrace duality: play and learning, imagination and reality.
- Childhood is recursive—growing, trying, and trying again.

## Practical Insights
- Use feedback: listen to your feelings and those who care for you.
- Restoration: mistakes are part of learning and fun.
- Agency: your choices matter and shape your story.

## Inspiration
- You are the hero of your own adventure. The world is full of stories waiting for you to explore.
