# Guide for Entrepreneurs

## Meta-Narrative Principles for Entrepreneurs
- Every idea is a seed—each venture a branching narrative.
- Embrace duality: risk and reward, vision and pragmatism.
- Entrepreneurship is recursive—iterate, learn, and adapt.

## Practical Insights
- Use feedback: test your ideas, listen to customers, and pivot as needed.
- Restoration: failures are data for future success.
- Agency: you create new realities through innovation.

## Inspiration
- You are a builder of futures. Your courage and creativity shape the world’s next chapter.
