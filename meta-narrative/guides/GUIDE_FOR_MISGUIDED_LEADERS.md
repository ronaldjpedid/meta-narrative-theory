# Guide for Misguided Leaders

## Meta-Narrative Principles for Misguided Leaders
- Every decision is a branch—each mistake, a chance for restoration.
- Embrace duality: power and humility, error and redemption.
- Leadership is recursive—growth comes from reflection and change.

## Practical Insights
- Use feedback: listen to those you lead and those you’ve harmed.
- Restoration: acknowledge mistakes and seek to repair trust.
- Agency: you can rewrite your story and the story of your community.

## Inspiration
- Redemption is always possible. True leadership is measured by the courage to change and restore what was lost.
