# Guide for Healthcare Workers

## Meta-Narrative Principles for Healthcare Workers
- Every act of care is a branch in the story of healing.
- Embrace duality: science and compassion, action and rest.
- Healthcare is recursive—each patient encounter shapes your growth.

## Practical Insights
- Use feedback: listen to patients, families, and your own well-being.
- Restoration: mistakes are opportunities for learning and empathy.
- Agency: your choices can restore hope and health.

## Inspiration
- You are a guardian of life’s narrative. Your work brings restoration to the world.
