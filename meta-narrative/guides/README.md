# Meta-Narrative Guides Index & Mission

This folder is dedicated to practical guides for people from all walks of life. Each guide translates the core principles of the meta-narrative theory into actionable insights, inspiration, and tools for a specific profession, craft, or life path.

## Purpose
- To make the meta-narrative theory accessible and useful to everyone, regardless of background or expertise.
- To provide tailored guidance for artists, makers, leaders, and everyday people.
- To foster creativity, meaning, and positive change in every field.
