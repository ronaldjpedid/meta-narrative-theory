# Guide for Artisans

## Meta-Narrative Principles for Artisans
- Every creation is a branch in the story of craft and tradition.
- Embrace duality: skill and inspiration, old and new.
- Crafting is recursive—each piece builds on experience and learning.

## Practical Insights
- Use feedback: refine your technique and learn from others.
- Restoration: flaws can become features—embrace imperfection.
- Agency: your hands shape beauty and meaning.

## Inspiration
- You are a bridge between past and future. Your work adds richness to the world’s story.
