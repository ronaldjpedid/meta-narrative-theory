# Guide for Students

## Meta-Narrative Principles for Students
- Every question is a branch—each answer, a new path.
- Embrace duality: curiosity and discipline, learning and unlearning.
- Your journey is recursive—growth comes from cycles of effort and reflection.

## Practical Insights
- Use feedback: seek help, review mistakes, and adapt your strategies.
- Restoration: setbacks are part of the learning story.
- Agency: your choices shape your future.

## Inspiration
- You are the hero of your own learning adventure. Every step forward matters.
