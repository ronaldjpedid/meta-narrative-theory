# Guide for Scientists

## Meta-Narrative Principles for Scientists
- Every hypothesis is a branch—each experiment a new path.
- Embrace duality: skepticism and curiosity, theory and observation.
- Science is recursive—knowledge grows through cycles of testing and revision.

## Practical Insights
- Use feedback: let data guide your conclusions.
- Restoration: errors are opportunities for deeper understanding.
- Agency: your discoveries expand the universe’s story.

## Inspiration
- You are an explorer of truth. Your work illuminates the unknown and inspires new questions.
